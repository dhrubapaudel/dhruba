About Me

I am Er. Dhruba Paudel, a Civil Engineer with a strong background in structural and geotechnical engineering. Currently pursuing my M.Sc. in Earthquake Engineering at IOE Thapathali Campus, Tribhuvan University, Nepal..

My graduate research focuses on the seismic performance of raft and Combined Pile Raft Foundation (CPRF) supported buildings considering Soil Structure Structure Interaction (SSSI), modelled using ABAQUS finite element software with the 2015 Gorkha Earthquake (Mw 7.8) as seismic input.

I am passionate about earthquake resilient design and contributing meaningfully to Nepal's built environment safety particularly in seismically active regions.
